Game Rules — Rock • Paper • Scissors

Welcome to the ultimate hand‑sign showdown! Learn the basics in seconds and start winning in style.

🔥 How to Play

  • Choose your move — Rock ✊, Paper 🖐️, or Scissors ✌️.
  • Face your opponent — challenge the computer 🤖
  • Reveal & score — the winner is decided instantly!

🏆 Who Beats Who?

  • 🪨 Rock crushes Scissors
  • ✂️ Scissors cut Paper
  • 📄 Paper covers Rock
  • 🤝 Same move? It’s a draw!

⭐ Extra Fun Rules

  • Play Best of 3, Best of 5, or create your own series.
  • Keep track of your wins and try to beat your high score.
  • It’s a mix of quick thinking and a little luck 🍀.

⚡ Fair Play

  • No cheats, no hacks — just pure fun.
  • Respect your opponent (even the sneaky AI 😉).
  • Most importantly — have fun! 🎉
