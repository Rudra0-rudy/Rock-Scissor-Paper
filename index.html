Rock paper scissors is a simple hand game usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. The possible shapes are rock (a fist), paper (an open hand), and scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V). The game has three possible outcomes other than a tie: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. The game is often used as a fair choosing method similar to drawing straws or flipping a coin. For the Game rule click on the Game Rules button above.
